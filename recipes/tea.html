Lemon Honey Tea

Description:

Citrusy sweet herbal tea to boost productivity.

Ingredients:

  • Lemon
  • Lipton black tea
  • Raw honey

Directions:

  1. Pour boiling water on tea bag. Steep for 3 minutes.

  2. Discard teabag and mix in 1 tablespoon of raw honey. Stir to combine.

  3. Add 1-2 tablespoons freh squeezed lemon juice. Stir to combine.

  4. Slice a lemon wheel and toss it in.
