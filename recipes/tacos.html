Tacos

Description:

Grilled chicken tacos.

Ingredients:

  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • 3x cans San Marzano tomatos
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Basil
  • Peccorino, paremseano, mozzarallea, riccotta
  • Eggs
  • Jumbo shell pasta

Directions:

Marinara Sauce:

  1. Finely dice the onion, celery, carrots, and garlic.

  2. Gently sweat the diced veggies in large dutch oven until softened and translucent in color.

  3. Add garlic, red chili flake and cook until fragrant. (~45 seconds)

  4. Add the 3 cans of San Marzano tomatos and 4 cups water. Stir thourughly and bring to a simmer.

  5. Use immersion blender to puree the sauce.

  6. Add basil and simmer the sauce for 3 hours, adding water if sauce becomes too thick.

  7. Season with salt, pepper to taste.

Stuffed Shells

  1. Boil pasta for 50% time on package directions. Drain, don't rinse.

  2. In a large bowl, mix riccotta, paremseano, peccorino, and grated mozzarella cheeses. Add 4 eggs, zest of one lemon, salt, pepper, and chopped parsley.

  3. Using a piping bag, stuff filling into shells.

  4. Layer sauce down in 9x13 baking tray. Place shells in tray.

  5. Drizzle with sauce and remaining cheeses.

  6. Cover in foil and bake at 350F for 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake uncovered for 15 minutes. Broil until cheese is toasted.
