Grilled Chicken Tacos

Description:

Grilled chicken tacos.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • Onion
  • Jalapeno
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Lime
  • Chipotle chili powder, garlic powder, smoked paprika, cumin
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded cheese

Directions:

  1. Season chicken breast with oil, spices, and a squeeze of lime juice.

  2. Dice and mix onion, jalapeno, garlic, cilantro, tomoto, and lime juice for quick pico de gallo. Season to taste with salt.

  3. Sear chicken on preheated cast iron pan until cooked to 165F internal temperature.

  4. Serve on warm tortillas with pico, cheese, and hot sauce.
